Key terms on this page

Below are some terms used on this page that are used often. Knowing these defintions may allow you to understand this page more.

More than 90% of people who are currently struggling with substance use disorders started before they were 18.[4]

Prevalence of use in the past year of selected substances among 12th graders, 2020

*Prevalence of use: The proportion of the population who has used a particular substance in a certain period. For example, if alcohol's prevalence of use within the past year is 40%, it means 40% of people used alcohol at least once in the past year.
