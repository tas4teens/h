Statistics

Here is a preview of SUD statistics in the US. For more in-depth statistics and better visuals, go to the Statistics page.

As of 2020, the yearly prevelance of use of marijuana is 35.2% amongst high school seniors.[1]

165.4 million people aged 12 and older have used a substance in the past month.[2]

70,360 people have died of drug overdoses in 2019.[3]

About 21 million Americans struggle with a substance use disorder.[4]

Out of these 21 million people, only 10% get proper treatment.[4]

More than 90% of people who are currently struggling with substance use disorders started before they were 18.[4]
