About Tas4Teens

What is Tas4Teens, and who is it created by?

Hi everyone! My name is Micaiah Cape, and I am a high school student who wants to raise awareness about adolescent substance use. This is the reason why I created Tas4Teens (the Truth About Substance Use, for Teens), which is a website dedicated to doing just that. This is an important topic, as most people who struggle with the disease of addiction started using substances when they were young (i.e. substance use can lead to addiction). A lot of adolescents may be exposed to these kind of substances at school or at home.

I lost my grandmother because my grandfather's addiction to smoking exposed my grandmother to so much second hand smoke to the point that she was diagnosed with lung cancer. After her death, I knew that the disease of addiction was one of the biggest problems the world faces today. I also learned that my grandfather started smoking when he was still an adolescent. These big realizations lead me to start this project, and I wanted to find a way to warn my peers about this big problem.

What does this website cover?

This website covers many topics, such as substance use disorders (SUD) in general, risk factors of SUD, how SUD works, statistics on adolescent substance use, and more. There are also separate pages that dive deep into specific substances. These pages would often consist of a short introduction to the substance, DSM-5 diagnosis (if any), the effects on the substance on the body and how the substance produces these effects, treatment (if any), and statistics. A bunch of the wording on this website is simplified so that even high schoolers can easily understand.

Also, its not just articles on this website! A "fun and games" section is coming soon, where timed quizzes, Jeopardy, and other games relating to facts about substance use can be played.
